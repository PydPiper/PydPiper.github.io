# Math Cheat Sheet

## How to compute the radius of curvature of an ellipse
[Ref wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellipse)

![ellipse](/_static/imgs/math/cheatsheet/ellipse.png)

$$ R(\theta) = {\frac{a^2}{b} \bigg(\frac{(1 - e^2 (2-e^2) cos(\theta)^2)}{(1-e^2cos(\theta)^2)}\bigg)^{3/2} } $$

where, e is the eccentricity 

$$ e = \sqrt{1-(b/a)^2} $$
